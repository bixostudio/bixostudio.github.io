Vikas Jakati

During his Cinematography college days worked with Celebrity Photographer Avinash Gowariker and was associated with Luxurious lifestyle Photographer Jaideep Oberoi whole he shot several hotel brands such as Ritz-Carlton, Taj, Four Seasons, and Jetwing Hotels. Post which, He has independently worked with numerous TVC, real estate brands, architects, manufacturing industries, and corporate videos.
