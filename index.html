about us

What Those Guys
can do for you

We tailor production to fit each client’s needs and while the content and purpose of a video is the driving force of every project, we recognize that our clients deserve a full service production that can manage a project from inception to completion. The process is what defines your time with us.

LIST OF SERVICES

Creative

At the heart of Those Guys, we understand that all communication starts with a great story, irrespective of what it is called, how long it is or how much it costs. That is the foundation of where creativity begins. At our core, Those Guys are not afraid to think over and above and beyond.

Production

With multi platform dedicated divisions and with the best and most talented technical and creative people, we are both flexible and versatile. Always innovating with new technology and working with not just a fine film in mind but an overall experience, production at Those Guys is key.

Post Production

With a team that has preditors at the editing table, storytelling, beautiful visuals and ears that are geared to complex yet subtle sound design and a VFX/CG team that can turn around a project in record time, the finishing touch of Those Guys is yet to be reckoned with.

Digital

Our freshly minted Digital team has already been producing some amazing films. Headed by Shachi Malhotra – both creative and direction, we help with ideation and execution of all your digital films with both time and budget in mind.

Producing is more than just putting together all the facets of production and execution, it is about the passion of storytelling. We are always pushing the boundaries creatively with the Directors and Agencies and pursuing to achieve great work
— Vivek Gupta

meet the founder

Vivek Gupta

a.k.a. the guy

Since beginning his career as a sound engineer 22 years ago, Vivek Gupta has immersed himself deeply in all elements of production. He has handled literally every aspect of the craft – from music and sound mixing, to editing; he’s been behind the camera, he’s worked as a producer as well as a post production head and finally heading production and post production, giving him an unparalleled knowledge of the most technical aspects of the production process in India and overseas. He has worked on television commercials, digital commercials, digital films and music videos as well as television shows for channels including MTV and Channel V. Vivek is obsessed with gadgets and the latest technology, and is always one step ahead of the curve. He is Those Guys’ safe pair of hands.

digital

Shachi Malhotra

a.k.a. the creative gal

Often known as the youngest one in the team, Shachi started her journey at the age of 19. By 21, she was working as a producer for brands like Rayban, Sony Music & Star sports.
An early change of career direction led her to pursue a short course at Prague film school.
Her enthusiasm and her passion for her vocation quickly led her to direct music videos for independent artists. After a year of film school, she immediately dove into the world of advertising and since then has directed for brands such as Godrej, Yfilms, Jack & Jones, MTV, Charmboard to name a few.
Having watched and followed all of Hayao Miyazaki’s work closely, Shachi loves films and concepts that require an eye for detail whether that’s in visuals or performance. Her aim is to build a character driven world with each project she works on. You will find her rescuing and treating animals on the streets when not working.

Filmmaking is like building a lego set. As long as you’ve got an idea of what you want to create, you can create it with all the different pieces you have in a box. Don’t follow the manual. Go out and build your own set!

— Shachi Malhotra

our team

Meet the rest of Those Guys

Kaizad Pardiwalla

Business Head

Kaizad got his experience with production during his theatre acting days, where he helped produce over 25 plays.

Kaizad moved to the world of digital in the early 2000s and led OgilvyOne to great success. His latest stint as head of Jack in the Box and COO of the 120 Media Collective group have him unparalleled insight into the ever-changing modern world of digital communication and knowledge to global trends.

He uses his experience across advertising, digital, video and theatre to build strong and lasting collaborations and partnerships with agencies and clients by ensuring quality and delivering value.

Preetha Iyer

Senior Producer

Preetha Iyer is our “chota packet – bada dhamaka”. Having started her career as a Production Assistant, knowledge of production is her forte. Her stint as an Associate EP on feature films gave her an added advantage which got her to move in to the advertising field. She was a DA to numerous Directors like Abhinav Pratiman, Nitish Sharma, Louis Carassco & Benaifer Malik. With almost 45 baby films under her hat, her progression to becoming a Producer was inevitable. She started Producing for Conversation Films, Elements and Boot Polissh. Now on board Those Guys Productions as a Partner – Senior Producer, she has both the creative and production bone that helps  manage projects from a very clear perspective.

Mitesh Mehta

Senior Producer

A seasoned Producer, with over a decade in the industry, Mitesh also brings his past experience as an Assistant Director & a Post Producer to the table. He has been called a “baby faced assassin”, “little Director” and “a Director’s delight”, given the value he adds to any job he produces. 

Known as “Mitz” to his buddies, he believes in teamwork and has joined Those Guys thanks to the company’s family feeling and warmth. Despite his diminutive size, Mitesh is actually a hardcore foodie and a walking Zomato, which comes in handy at lunchtime. He’s also a tech geek, OCD about cleanliness and a complete perfectionist, which tends to delight clients and directors alike.

Debdutta Saha

Executive Producer

AKA Debbie is from Kolkata and has lived in Mumbai and Delhi (currently moved back to Kolkata after getting married to the love of her life). She started her career with Leo Burnett and then moved on to Dentsu and then moved on to work with Agencies like Rediffusion & Chimp&Z. Debbie is a huge foodie who also dabbles in boutique jewellery in her spare time and is our Executive Producer based out of Kolkata.
